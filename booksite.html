Benefits of Reading

Reading books is very beneficial because it improves our vocabulary, and verbal-linguistic skills, it also reduces stress, increases empathy and it also aids sleep. Reading books is a natural hobby of mine however this hobby can be developed by anyone. Reading books also instills many good qualities, especially in children like - punctuality and discipline, etc. Reading books is a hobby of many successful people like - Bill Gates, Elon Musk, etc. Reading books has changed the lives of many people. It is essential to make a person successful in life.
